Isaac Ryu's Biography

I am majoring in Broadcast Digital Journalism and minoring in Information Management & Technology. I am interested in sports journalism with a focus infusing statistics into a unique style of coverage.

I am currently an independent contractor for the Orlando Magic Daily and Rip City Project at FanSided writing articles covering games, trades and news.

Prior to working for FanSided, I interned at DBLTAP, an esports news site which is a sister site to FanSided. While I do write about the Orlando Magic and the Portland Trailblazers, my true allegiance lies with the New York Knicks.
